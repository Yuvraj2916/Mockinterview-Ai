# Mockinterview-Ai
HireMind AI is an agentic AI-powered job search and mock interview platform that combines LinkedIn-style job tracking with adaptive, real-time AI interview simulation and performance analytics.  Built to help students, fresh graduates, and job switchers prepare for interviews with intelligent, personalized feedback.

## 💡 The Vision

Job hunting and interview prep are fundamentally broken. Candidates blast resumes into the void and step into technical interviews completely blind. 

**HireMindAi** bridges the gap. We provide a seamless ecosystem where candidates can discover roles, optimize their resumes using AI, and—most importantly—practice for the exact job they are applying for using our **Live AI Video Interviewer**. The platform dynamically reads a candidate's uploaded resume—such as recognizing a machine learning project involving audio feature extraction—and generates highly specific, adaptive interview questions on the fly.

## ✨ Core Features

* **🎙️ Live AI Mock Interviews (The Killer Feature):** A real-time, camera-enabled interview environment. The AI dynamically adapts its questions based on your real-time responses and confidence levels.
* **🧠 Smart Resume Hub:** Upload your PDF/DOCX. Our AI instantly parses it, extracts your technical skills into chips, generates a 1-line elevator pitch, and scores your match against specific job descriptions.
* **🔍 Integrated Job Board:** A sleek, LinkedIn-style job discovery page featuring advanced filtering and 1-click apply/mock-interview triggers.
* **📊 Analytics Dashboard:** Track your application pipeline and view post-interview performance reports, highlighting weak areas (e.g., System Design vs. Behavioral) with actionable next steps.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** Next.js 14 (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Framer Motion
* **Backend / AI:** Vercel AI SDK, OpenAI GPT-4o
* **Database & Auth:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
* **Hardware Integration:** WebRTC / HTML5 MediaDevices API (for live camera/mic feed)
